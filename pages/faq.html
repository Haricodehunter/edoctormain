People with disabilities

Does Abu Dhabi have special needs facilities?

Most of Abu Dhabi's five-star hotels, malls and heritage attractions have wheelchair facilities. Wheelchair ramps often have steep angles. Abu Dhabi International Airport is well equipped for physically challenged travellers. There is a special check-in gate with direct access from the car park, as well as dedicated lifts, and a meet and assist service.
